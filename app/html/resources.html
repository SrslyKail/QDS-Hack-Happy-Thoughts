Mental Health Crisis Resources

If you're experiencing a crisis or need urgent support, please contact one of the following services:

Provincial Health Services Authority (PHSA)

PHSA offers a range of mental health support services. Visit their website for more information.

Crisis Centre of BC

Suicide hotline: 1-800-SUICIDE (1-800-784-2433) - Available 24/7, free of charge.

BC Mental Health Support Line

Heretohelp: 310-6789 - No area code needed, free and available 24/7.

Canadian Suicide Prevention Crisis Helpline

Call or text 9-8-8 - Available 24/7 nationwide.

Here2Talk

Connects post-secondary students with mental health support. Available 24/7 via app, phone, and web.

Canada-wide toll-free: 1-877-857-3397

Canada-wide direct: 604-642-5212

KUU-US Indigenous Crisis Line

Toll Free: 1-800-588-8717

- A 24/7 service for Indigenous Peoples in B.C.

Youth in BC

Online chat is available from Noon to 1 AM in BC and Yukon only for youth 25 and under, click to chat
